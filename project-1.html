What is UNXPLND?

Unxplnd.market is a thriving brand based in Indonesia, and we were excited to explore new possibilities for their UI design.
Exploration result of UI Design for a brand in Indonesia @unxplnd.market.

There are 6 sections in this website design:

~ The first part of the design features a navbar with the logo on the left side, a menu button beside it, and login, search, and cart icons on the right side of the navbar. After that, there is a hero section in the form of a promotional banner with a product image and a call-to-action button that invites users to start shopping for the product.

~ The second section features a product section in a tidy and simple grid format, with trending products and featured categories.

~ The third section displays featured products in a neat grid format. Each product is shown with a model image, a title, a brief description, and a price, as well as a button for users to purchase or just add to the cart.

~ The fourth section features a product display in banner form that includes a model image, a title, a brief description, and a price. It is made to pique the interest of users and get them to look at other products.

~ The fifth part is a collection of articles that have been or will be released in a grid format.

~ Finally, the footer section includes links to important pages such as the about page, contact page, and social media links. The footer also displays payment and security badges to build trust with the users.
